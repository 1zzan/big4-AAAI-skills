## Verification Procedure

1. Open the current-year venue page from the official anchor.
2. Locate the current CFP and author instructions.
3. Confirm deadlines, submission portal, page limits, anonymity, ethics, artifact, supplementary-material, AI-use, rebuttal, and camera-ready rules.
4. If a local skill conflicts with current official instructions, follow the official instructions.
